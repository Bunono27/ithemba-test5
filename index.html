Who We Are

History & Vision

Ithemba was founded in 1988 under the umbrella of Mental Health in Port Elizabeth. We became independent in 2014, and our new management team took over in 2023 with a renewed vision.

The name "Ithemba" means Hope. We are an Intellectual and Physical Disability Centre catering to children with Down syndrome, Autism, and Cerebral Palsy. Our facility is fully wheelchair-friendly.

What We Do

  • Ages 0 - 18: Tailored care for children and youth.
  • Specialized Care: For Intellectual and Physical disabilities.
  • Support: Equipping children with skills and supporting parents.
